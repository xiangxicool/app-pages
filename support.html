FAQ

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to the app's settings or pricing screen and tap "Restore Purchases." This will restore any previous purchases linked to your Apple ID.

How do I cancel a subscription?

Open iPhone Settings → tap your name → Subscriptions → select the app → Cancel Subscription. Apple manages all subscription billing.

The app isn't working properly.

Try closing and reopening the app, or restarting your device. If the issue persists, email us with a description of the problem and your device model.

How do I request a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com to request a refund for any purchase.

Is my data safe?

Yes. Most of our apps work entirely offline and do not collect or transmit any personal data. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Which devices are supported?

Our apps support iPhone and iPad running iOS 16.0 or later.
